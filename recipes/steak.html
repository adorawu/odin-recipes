Steak

Description

this is a delicious recipe for reverse seared steak. Restaurant quality for half the price.

Ingredients

  • steak
  • salt
  • pepper

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees
    2. Season steak generously with salt and pepper
    3. Cook steak in oven for 25 min (for med rare)
    4. Take out and let rest for 15 min
    5. Sear in a smoking pan with a little oil for about 1-2 min per side
    6. Enjoy :)
